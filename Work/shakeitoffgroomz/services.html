Shake It Off Cat Groomz Services

At Shake it Off Cat Groomz LLC, I offer a range of professional grooming services for your furry friend. My services include bathing, hair styling, nail trimming, and more.

These additional resources below will provide you with a more in-depth understanding of the specific grooming options available for your furry friends. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact me. I am always happy to help.

Basic Services

Home Visit

At Shake it Off Cat Groomz LLC, I offer a home visit service to determine whether my in-home grooming service is a good fit for you and your furry friend. The purpose of the home visit is to assess the suitability of your home for my grooming services and to ensure that your cat is comfortable with my grooming.

Grooming Service

kitten wash and trim

At Shake it Off Cat Groomz LLC, I understand that some kittens may be nervous or fearful during the grooming process. That's why I offer the Kitten Wash & Trim Exposure service, which is designed to gently ease your kitten into the grooming process and keep them comfortable.

The Kitten Wash & Trim Exposure service is suitable for kittens aged 9 weeks to 6 months and includes a variety of grooming activities designed to introduce your kitten to the grooming process. These activities may include nail trimming, ear cleaning, brushing, bath and dryer exposure, and clipper exposure.

To ensure the safety and well-being of your kitten during the grooming process, I require that all kittens have received their rabies vaccine at four months of age or older. The goal of this service is to help your kitten become comfortable with the grooming process and to establish positive associations with grooming activities. I am trained to work gently and patiently with kittens, using techniques and tools that are safe and appropriate for their age and size.

Waterless bath

The Waterless Bath is a quick and convenient option that uses a specially formulated shampoo that doesn't require water. We apply the shampoo to your pet's coat, work it in with a towel or brush, and then remove excess shampoo with a towel or dryer. This service also includes a brush out, ear cleaning, and nail trim.

For an all-around grooming experience, try our Waterless Bath and Trim. This service includes all the benefits of the Waterless Bath, with the addition of trimming your pet's fur, nails, and paw pads. Additionally, you have the option to add on trimming of the ears, feet, and sanitary area to further enhance your pet's appearance and hygiene.

All our waterless services are customizable to meet your pet's specific needs, and are ideal for pets who are anxious or fearful of water, have mobility issues, or for quick cleanups between traditional grooming sessions. Whether you choose the Waterless Bath or the Waterless Bath and Trim, you can rest assured that your pet will be left looking and feeling their best without the need for water.

Lion Clip

A full grooming service that involves shaving your cat's coat down to a short length all over their body.

The Lion Clip is a stylish and popular grooming style that is suitable for cats with long or medium-length coats. It involves shaving your cat's coat down to a short length, typically between 1.5 mm to 1/2 inches, all over their body. This style is often referred to as a "lion cut," as it gives your cat a wild and regal appearance.

In addition to the Lion Clip, this service includes nail trimming, ear cleaning, and a complimentary bath and blow out. You can also request a toe tuff, which involves trimming the fur on your cat's toes to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Asian Lion Cut (Dino Clip)

A full grooming service that combines elements of the Asian Clip and the Lion Clip.

The Asian Lion Cut (Dino Clip) is a stylish and unique grooming style that involves shaving the back legs and lower back end area of your cat's body, while leaving the fur in the front and middle section of the body intact. This style is ideal for cats with long or medium-length coats that may be prone to matting or tangles in the shaved areas. It can also help to eliminate most of the problem areas that long-haired or older cats may have.

In addition to the Asian Lion Cut (Shaving), this service includes nail trimming and ear cleaning. You can also choose from a variety of optional tail styles, including an unshaved tail or a pom pom.

Dematt Hair Clip

A full grooming service that focuses on removing matting from your cat's coat.

The Dematt Hair Clip is suitable for cats with long or medium-length coats that have become matted or tangled. It involves a thorough grooming session that focuses on removing matting from your cat's coat, using specialized combing and brushing techniques. This service helps to keep your cat's coat healthy and looking its best, and can also help to prevent future matting.

In addition to the Dematt Hair Clip, this service includes nail trimming, ear cleaning, and a complimentary bath and blow out. These activities help to keep your cat looking and feeling their best, while also maintaining their coat health and appearance.

Plush Trim

A full grooming service that leaves a varying length of fur on your cat's body.

The Plush Trim is suitable for cats with long or medium-length coats and involves a trim that leaves a varying length of fur, typically between 1/2 inch to 1 inch, on your cat's body. This style is perfect for cats that enjoy a more natural or wild look, or for cats that have thick, dense coats that may benefit from a shorter length.

In addition to the Plush Trim, this service includes nail trimming, ear cleaning, brushing, a bath, and a blow out. These activities help to keep your cat looking and feeling their best, while also maintaining their coat health and appearance.

Asian Clip

A full grooming service that leaves a specific pattern of fur on your cat's body.

The Asian Clip is a stylish and unique grooming style that involves leaving a jacket of fur on your cat's back and legs, while the rest of their body is shaved down to a short length. This style is often referred to as a "jacket and pants" look, as it resembles a human jacket and pants.

In addition to the Asian Clip, this service includes nail trimming, ear cleaning, brushing, and a complimentary bath and blow out. These activities help to keep your cat looking and feeling their best, while also maintaining their coat health and appearance.

Bath only

Hairless Cat

I offer a specialized bathing service for hairless cats. As these cats do not have a protective coat of fur, it is important to keep their skin clean and moisturized to prevent dryness and irritation.

During my bathing service for hairless cats, I use gentle, pH-balanced shampoos and conditioners specifically formulated for their sensitive skin. I also pay special attention to the areas around their ears, eyes, and nose, as these areas can be particularly delicate. This service also includes a between toe cleansing massage.

After the bath, I carefully dry your hairless cat using a towel.

My bathing service for hairless cats is designed to keep their skin clean, healthy, and well-moisturized, while also providing a comfortable and stress-free experience. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact me. I am always happy to help.

My bathing service for short-haired cats is designed to keep their coat clean, healthy, and well-groomed, while also providing a comfortable and stress-free experience. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact me. I am always happy to help.
