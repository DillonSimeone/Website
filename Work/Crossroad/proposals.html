Google Ads

Google Ads can be an effective way to drive traffic to your website and attract potential customers, but they are not a silver bullet solution that can solve all of your business problems. Here are a few reasons why:

  • Limited reach:

    While Google Ads can be effective for reaching people who are actively searching for your products or services, they may not be as effective for reaching people who are not actively looking for what you offer. This means you may be missing out on potential customers who are not currently in the market for your products or services.

  • Ad fatigue and ad blocking:

    Even if your ads are effective at first, over time people may start to tune them out or actively avoid them. This is known as "ad fatigue," and it can be exacerbated by the increasing number of people who use ad blockers. In fact, as of 2021, over 27% of internet users worldwide use ad blockers, which means your ads may not even be seen by a significant portion of your potential audience.

  • Competition:

    There may be a lot of competition for Google Ads. This means you may need to spend more money to get your ads seen by the right people, and even then you may not be able to compete with larger businesses with bigger advertising budgets.

Things Dillon could do to improve the business's likelihood of success

Backlink outreach

Backlinks from other websites can improve your website's search engine ranking and drive more traffic to your site. I can identify relevant websites in your industry and reach out to them to request a backlink in exchange for a reciprocal link on your website.

Content marketing

Creating high-quality, informative content that is relevant to your client's target audience can help attract new customers and build trust with existing ones. This could involve creating blog posts, videos, or other types of content that provide valuable information, tips, or insights related to your client's industry. You could also consider guest posting on other websites or partnering with industry influencers to reach a wider audience.

HTML Emails for marketing email campaigns

A well-designed and targeted email campaign can be an effective way to reach potential customers and promote your business. I can design and send HTML emails that are customized to your brand and include clear calls-to-action to encourage conversions.

Affiliate marketing

With affiliate marketing, you would partner with other businesses or individuals who would promote your products or services to their own audiences. This can help you tap into new customer segments and benefit from increased brand awareness. The best part is, you only pay your affiliates when they generate a sale for you, making it a cost-effective marketing strategy.

Social media advertising

Social media platforms like Facebook provide powerful advertising tools that can help you reach a highly targeted audience. I can create and manage advertising campaigns on Facebook and other social media platforms that are tailored to your business and audience.
