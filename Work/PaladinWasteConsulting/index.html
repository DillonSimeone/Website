Frequently Asked Questions

How does shared savings work? +
If we find savings, we share a percentage of those savings with you for a specified period. If we don't find savings, you owe us nothing.
Do I have to switch waste providers? +
Not necessarily. We often negotiate better rates with your current provider. Switching is only recommended if it offers significantly better value.
What info do you need to start? +
A copy of your most recent waste bill and your current service contract is all we need to perform an initial analysis.
