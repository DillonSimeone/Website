HapticBlaze: Telemetry Engine

A unified Hardware Abstraction Layer (HAL) and captive portal for linear resonant actuators (LRAs), ERMs, and solenoid drivers. HapticBlaze features dynamic back-EMF resonance tracking, a 32-band real-time audio FFT mapping module for acoustic sensory substitution, a high-frequency WebSocket telemetry stream, and a live sandboxed web simulator.
