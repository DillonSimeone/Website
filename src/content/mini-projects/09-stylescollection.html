Style & Aesthetic Hub

An interactive, visual catalog exploring 50 distinct web aesthetics and design systems. It covers everything from Neobrutalism's high-contrast borders and Swiss editorial typography grids, to nostalgic Windows 95 retro desktops, vaporwave sunsets, and claymorphic 3D surfaces. Each aesthetic is a fully-realized layout highlighting the harmonious pairing of curated typography, color palettes, and motion design.
