Sonic Agency: DHH STEAM Curriculum

A comprehensive K-12 bilingual ASL/English sensory STEAM curriculum designed for Deaf and Hard of Hearing (DHH) students. Developed under CymaSpace and aligned with a National Endowment for the Arts (NEA) grant, it utilizes CyberDecks, haptic vests, and wrist gesture sensors to translate acoustic frequency spectra, beats, and classified ASL hand shapes directly into localized physical tactile vibrations.
