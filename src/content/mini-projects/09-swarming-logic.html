Swarming Logic

An interactive exploration of Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO). This project visualizes how individual agents (particles) cooperate to find the global "best" solution.

The swarm dynamically reacts to your mouse movements, illustrating complex emergent behavior through simple mathematical rules.

  • Swarm 1: Basic Proximity - Blue particles reactive to mouse presence.
  • Swarm 2: Wrapping - Continuous movement with screen wrapping.
  • Swarm 3: Chromatic - Introducing multicolored particle variety.
  • Swarm 4: Gravity - Particles gravitating toward a central target.
  • Swarm 5: Image Swarm - Transitioning to image-based particles (Shrek) with mouse following.
  • Swarm 6: Flocking - Complex flocking behavior and target circling.
  • Swarm 7: High Density - Optimized high-density flocking with teleporting targets.
  • Swarm 8: PSO Engine - Full Particle Swarm Optimization logic powered by p5.js.
