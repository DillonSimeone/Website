Library of Babel

An immersive, interactive 3D spatial interface experiment designed to make browsing my past embedded projects and AI swarms completely natural. The visualizer maps 62 real microcontroller repositories I've written in the past alongside 20 autonomous hardware startup blueprints synthesized by Claude Code AI swarms during the Oregon Hackathon. By mapping project metrics to shelf coordinates and volume thickness, it creates a cozy, 3D bookcase browse environment—similar in spirit to the Universe 3D skyscraper city visualizer.
