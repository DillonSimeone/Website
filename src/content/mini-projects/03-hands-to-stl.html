Hands to STL

Ever wanted to 3D print a custom hand sculpture without losing your mind in Blender? This tool uses MediaPipe's hand tracking to capture your hand pose from a webcam and converts it directly into a printable STL file. Multiple artistic styles available: solid bones, winding ribbons, crystalline formations, and organic metaball blobs.

Perfect for making custom jewelry displays, ASL letter sculptures, or just freezing a gesture in time without modeling skills.
