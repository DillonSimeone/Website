# esp32c6-hw — Cost-Down Summary
- **Project Purpose:** Demo on ESP32-C6 dev kit (OLED + HC-SR04 + NeoPixel). Project exists specifically to exercise C6 silicon (WiFi6/Thread/Matter); migration off-family defeats its purpose.
- **Original MCU:** ESP32-C6 (`esp32-c6-devkitc-1`).
- **Recommended Replacement:** **Keep ESP32-C6-WROOM-1-N8** (LCSC C5736265). Cost-down only by moving from dev kit to bare module on custom PCB.
- **Cost Delta ($):** −$3.05 vs. dev-kit (module $1.95 vs ~$5 dev kit), $0.00 silicon swap.
- **Confidence Level (1-10):** 10 — locked to family by purpose.
