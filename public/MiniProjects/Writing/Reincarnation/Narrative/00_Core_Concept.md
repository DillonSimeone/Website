# Core Concept

Let start writing a story. 

A human of the 21th century dies in a mugging. This was a programmer, well on his way to early retirement. He worked mainly with machine learning, but didn't really have any attachments to the world; no family,  and friends. But he barely noticed, he was always so interested in just endlessly building.

He is reincarnated into a different world with his memories intact, as a demon. Instead of born, he just suddenly came to awareness in a demonic underling, with the new body's memories merging with his memories.

It wasn't even a battle of will, he became the dominant personality without trying as the demon faded into background noise. The first thing on his mind is investigating magic... The previous owner of this body did not appreciate magic!

Almost overnight, from just applying his programming knowledge by optimizing magic beyond anything the world have achieved, he became the most powerful demon... But no one catches on!

Up until the hero party attacked him for the first time. His normal duties was to just be one of the guards on the way to the demon lord, meant to, at best, defeat the party, or at worse, delay the party by giving up his life.

He defeats the party by firing off a tiny mote of spell that quickly unfolds into something on the verge of sentience. Instead of outright being killed, the party was taken prisoner by the mote of magic, and the demon starts panicking.

What do I do with those humans? Despite being somewhat cold-hearted, and antisocial, he believes that killing always is more trouble than it's worth unless you go all the way through... He thinks back on the demon's memories, and gets a vague understanding of how much humans are out there.

Yep, not worth the trouble of killing.

In the end, he was able to sneak the party out of the castle after putting them to sleep. 

The party comes back. And is defeated again.

Eventually, the demon comes to understand that he's actually many times more powerful than even the demon lord and the entire demonic armies combined. His application of magic goes beyond arcane and exotic. He have basically became the avatar of magic, with most of his organs long turned into mana. Magic begins and ends at him nowadays.

He overall thinks that the demon race is embarrassing in how base and violent they are overall, and almost thinks the same of humans. But he understands that's just due to how little experience he has with the human race. So he start negotiating with the hero party the next time they show up.

He was convinced that his attention should be turned toward humanity. He walks out of the castle with the human party, saying to trust him and just follow him for now.

He snaps his finger.

The castle is now gone along with everyone within. There wasn't even a blast, it was as if they were teleported. He explains that they're all now sealed in an orb in the field now, time-frozen. And the orb is covered with self-repairing barriers. Problem solved. Now to investigate if this world's humanity is worth the effort. 

The party is afraid of what they just let out of the bottle.
