# Style Collection Documentation

This directory contains experimental explorations of modern styling alternatives to traditional glassmorphism.

## 1. Neobrutalism
- **Core Aesthetic**: High contrast, vibrant colors (pure cyan, hot pink, vivid yellow, bright orange), heavy/thick solid black borders (e.g. `border: 4px solid #000`), offset sharp shadows (`box-shadow: 6px 6px 0px #000`), sans-serif thick typography, and quirky/irregular layouts.
- **Vibe**: Raw, rebellious, playful.

## 2. Cyberpunk Neon / Synthwave
- **Core Aesthetic**: Deep dark theme (near-black, dark violet/navy), neon outlines (pink, purple, cyan), heavy text and box glows (`filter: drop-shadow`, `box-shadow`), retro-futuristic grid backgrounds, monospace or clean tech typography, and status indicators.
- **Vibe**: High-tech, digital-futurism.

## 3. Minimalist Editorial / Swiss Grid
- **Core Aesthetic**: Pure black & white or highly muted palette, serif headings combined with clean geometric sans-serif body text, spacious typography leading, asymmetric layouts based on clean grids, and hairline borders.
- **Vibe**: High-end editorial print, sophisticated, stark.

## 4. Claymorphism / Soft 3D
- **Core Aesthetic**: Soft pastel/gradient backgrounds, panels with rounded corners, double internal shadows (`inset` shadows to simulate inflation/clay depth) combined with a soft, blurry drop shadow.
- **Vibe**: Friendly, interactive, toy-like.

## 5. Retro Terminal / Phosphor CRT
- **Core Aesthetic**: Monospace amber or green phosphor typography on true-black, retro scanline overlays, simulated CRT glass curvature, retro command inputs, blink animation cursors, and pixelated borders.
- **Vibe**: Nostalgic, hacker-terminal, vintage computing.

## 6. Art Deco / Gatsby Luxe
- **Core Aesthetic**: Deep dark matte background paired with metallic gold framing lines, symmetrical geometric borders, and high-end serif typography (e.g. *Cinzel*).
- **Vibe**: 1920s vintage elegance, high-end luxury.

## 7. Y2K Chrome Acid
- **Core Aesthetic**: Flashing grid backdrops, liquid metal chrome radial gradients, neon lime green and magenta fills, wide stretched type (*Syne*), and starburst icons.
- **Vibe**: Retro cyber-rave culture, early 2000s tech-futurism.

## 8. Organic Botanical
- **Core Aesthetic**: Soft terracotta, sage greens, sandstone background colors, shifting fluid shapes (`border-radius` morphing), and textured paper grains.
- **Vibe**: Artisanal, ecological, calming.

## 9. Windows 95 Retro Desktop
