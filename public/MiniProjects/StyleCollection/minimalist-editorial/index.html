The Editorial Grid

An exercise in structural simplicity. Where whitespace acts as structure, and typography commands attention.

GRID COLS:
01 / PRINCIPLES

Whitespace as a Design Element

In standard web design, space is often filled to maximize information density. In editorial design, empty space is deliberate. It focuses the viewer's attention, establishes clear groupings, and allows elements to breathe.

02 / TYPOGRAPHY

Stark Contrast & Font Pairing

This layout pairs the highly expressive, sharp serifs of Cormorant Garamond for headers with the clean, neutral readability of Inter for body text. Height, weight, and style contrasts create a natural reading rhythm without color overhead.
