Not Vibrating?

If the status says "Success" but you feel nothing:

  1. Go to Settings > Sound & vibration > Vibration & haptics.
  2. Ensure Touch feedback (or Interactive haptics) is ON and sliders are MAXED.
  3. Ensure Media vibration is also ON.
  4. Try toggling Adaptive vibration off/on.
