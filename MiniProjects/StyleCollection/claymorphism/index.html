Claymorphism

Friendly, inflated 3D shapes and squishy tactile interactions.

What is Claymorphism?

Unlike flat design or glassmorphism, claymorphism uses multiple inner shadows and soft outer drop shadows to simulate inflated 3D objects made of clay or plastic.
