Sundered Minds

The Dreaming Sector Xenobiological reports on the shattering of spacetime during the first human psionic contact and the fractals that remained. The Wall Containment brief for Sector 7-Null, where Dara Osei's "lizard brain" took over a Qeth'vara communion and became "The Lizard That Ate The Sky". The Egress Final testimony of Prof. Edmund Harwick concerning the true habitation of the Elder Ones—folded within the human brainstem. The Falling Star How the Vel'Oshari Communion was sundered by the fire of a single human programmer's mind during a routine workday prank. The Hunger An incident report on Marcus Webb, the human programmer who accidentally refactored a Xel'thari collective during a neural bridge prank. The Singularity (Veth) A testimony on the Veth Collective's neural bridge prank that accidentally birthed a machine god and led to the Final Cause. The Decend (Xel'thari) The account of Marcus Webb indexing 400,000 Xel'thari minds and invoking a latent daemon.
